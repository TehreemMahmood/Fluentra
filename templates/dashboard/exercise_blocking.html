Tips for Managing Blocks

  • Pause & Breathe: When you feel a block coming, pause briefly and take a gentle breath before continuing.
  • Easy Onset: Start words with a soft, gentle voice rather than forcing them out.
  • Light Contact: Keep your lips, tongue, and jaw relaxed—avoid tensing up on difficult sounds.
  • Slide Into Words: Try stretching the first sound slightly to ease into the word smoothly.

Focus Areas

Tongue twisters help you practice difficult sound combinations. Focus on plosive consonants (P, B, T, D, K, G) which often trigger blocks.

Progress Goal

Aim to complete each level with 80%+ accuracy. Consistent daily practice of 10-15 minutes yields the best results for managing blocks.
