Stutter Type Exercises

Choose a stutter type to practice targeted tongue twisters.

01

Repetition

Practice smooth transitions between sounds to reduce repetitions like "b-b-ball" or "I-I-I want". Focus on gentle onset and continuous airflow.
02

Prolongation

Work on controlled sound duration to manage prolongations like "ssssnake" or "fffffish". Master smooth, flowing speech patterns.
03

Blocking

Practice smooth airflow and gentle onset to overcome blocks on sounds like "k", "g", "t", "d". Release tension naturally.
04

Interjection

Practice fluent phrasing to reduce filler words like "um", "uh", "like", "you know". Build confident, clear speech.
