Bridging the Gap in Speech Therapy

We're on a mission to make speech therapy accessible, affordable, and effective for everyone.

Our Story

FluEntra was founded in 2023 by a team of speech pathologists and AI researchers who saw a massive gap in accessible care. Millions of people struggle with stuttering, but therapist availability and cost are major barriers.

We built FluEntra to provide a bridge—high-quality, data-driven feedback that people can use anytime, anywhere, to maintain their progress between professional sessions.
